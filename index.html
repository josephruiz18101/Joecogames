Our Games

Neon Hunters

Step into the dark alleys and neon-lit streets of a broken world where monstrous bio-mechanical creatures roam free. Neon Hunters is a fast-paced, 2.5D cyberpunk action game blending intense monster hunting with deep exploration and gritty storytelling. Play as an elite Hunter in a sprawling, dystopian city teetering on the edge of collapse. Track, fight, and defeat terrifying creatures born from failed experiments and rogue tech. Salvage their parts, upgrade your gear, and forge your legacy.
