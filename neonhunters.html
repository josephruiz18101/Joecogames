Neon Hunters

Step into the dark alleys and neon-lit streets of a broken world where monstrous bio-mechanical creatures roam free. Neon Hunters is a fast-paced, 2.5D cyberpunk action game blending intense monster hunting with deep exploration and gritty storytelling. Play as an elite Hunter in a sprawling, dystopian city teetering on the edge of collapse. Track, fight, and defeat terrifying creatures born from failed experiments and rogue tech. Salvage their parts, upgrade your gear, and forge your legacy. 🧪 Explore Biomes: From glowing alleyways to toxic swamps and underground labs, the city is alive—and dangerous. ⚔️ Skill-Based Combat: Dodge, slash, and shoot your way through enemies with fluid combos and manual controls. 🧬 Craft & Customize: Build powerful weapons, armor, and even tame companion beasts from fallen monsters. 💀 Face Nightmarish Bosses: Each zone holds terrifying apex monsters—only the strongest Hunters survive. 🧠 Unravel the Mystery: Why are these monsters here? Who is behind the chaos? Uncover secrets in a world where technology and biology collide.
