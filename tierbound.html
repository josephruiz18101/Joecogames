Enter the World of Tierbound

Step into a world where every expedition is a new adventure. Tierbound is a 3D action roguelite that combines fast-paced combat, rewarding exploration, deep crafting, and endless replayability.

Journey through handcrafted biomes filled with dangerous monsters, hidden bosses, rare resources, and powerful loot as you uncover the mysteries of a world on the brink of collapse.

Play as one of several unique classes, each with its own abilities, playstyle, and progression. Fight your way through challenging encounters, collect valuable materials, craft legendary equipment, and grow stronger with every run.

Game Features

🌿 Explore Handcrafted Biomes

Venture through forests, caves, ancient ruins, frozen peaks, volcanic wastelands, and other dangerous environments, each hiding unique enemies, resources, and secrets.

⚔️ Skill-Based Combat

Master responsive combat with class abilities, dodging, positioning, and carefully timed attacks to defeat increasingly powerful foes.

🛠️ Craft & Upgrade

Gather materials from monsters and the environment to forge stronger weapons, armor, consumables, and equipment that shape your playstyle.

👹 Challenge Powerful Bosses

Face massive bosses and discover rare hidden encounters that reward exclusive crafting materials and legendary gear.

📖 Build Your Character

Unlock new skills, specialize your build, and experiment with different combinations of weapons, abilities, and equipment to create your perfect adventurer.

🔁 Endless Replayability

Every expedition offers new opportunities, hidden paths, valuable discoveries, and challenging encounters, ensuring that no two runs ever feel the same.
