Help Build Tierbound

Tierbound is being developed by an independent studio with one goal: to create an action roguelite that rewards exploration, progression, and discovery.

Every contribution helps bring the world of Tierbound to life. Your support goes directly toward game development, allowing us to invest in better art, animations, music, software, and the talented people who help make this vision possible.

Whether it's $5 or $500, every donation helps us move one step closer to launching the game we've always dreamed of creating.

Why We're Building Tierbound

JoeCoGames was founded on the belief that great games reward curiosity, creativity, and mastery.

Tierbound isn't just another roguelite — it's a world designed to be explored. We want every run to feel meaningful, every boss memorable, and every piece of gear worth earning.

Our goal is to create a game players can come back to for hundreds of hours while constantly discovering something new.
